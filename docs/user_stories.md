## Season 1 – Core Reporting (Sales & Customers)

Focus: foundational reporting for sales, customers, and plants; basic SELECTs, JOINs, GROUP BY.

### User stories

- **US-S1-CEO**  
  As a CEO, I want a monthly revenue and order volume summary by region and plant so that I can see where demand is growing or shrinking.

- **US-S1-SALES-1**  
  As a Sales Director, I want a list of top 50 customers by revenue and margin so that I can prioritize account reviews.

- **US-S1-SALES-2**  
  As a Sales Director, I want a list of new vs existing customers so that I can tailor outreach and retention efforts.

- **US-S1-PLANT-1**  
  As a Plant Manager, I want a weekly list of open orders by due date and plant so that I can plan staffing.

## Season 2 – Operations, Inventory, and Suppliers

Focus: operations analytics across inventory, supply chain, and production; joins, CTEs, window functions, and views.

### User stories

- **US-S2-SC-1**  
  As a Supply Chain Manager, I want to see products at risk of stockout in the next 30 days so that I can expedite POs or adjust production plans.

- **US-S2-SC-2**  
  As a Supply Chain Manager, I want days-of-supply and safety stock indicators by product and plant so that I can balance stockouts against overstock.

- **US-S2-PROC-1**  
  As a Procurement Manager, I want supplier scorecards showing on-time delivery and cost trends so that I can negotiate or switch suppliers.

- **US-S2-PROC-2**  
  As a Procurement Manager, I want to rank suppliers by cost and reliability so that I can choose preferred suppliers for key components.

- **US-S2-PLANT-2**  
  As a Plant Manager, I want to identify lines with poor schedule adherence and low yield so that I can reduce overtime and scrap.

- **US-S2-PLANT-3**  
  As a Plant Manager, I want to understand utilization by plant and product family so that I can redistribute work and reduce bottlenecks.

## Season 3 – Profitability, Scenarios, and Executive View

Focus: profitability, scenarios, and cross-system executive views; recursive CTEs, summary tables, and more advanced SQL.

### User stories

- **US-S3-CFO-1**  
  As a CFO, I want fully loaded margin by product so that I can decide which SKUs to discontinue or reprice.

- **US-S3-CFO-2**  
  As a CFO, I want customer-level profitability after discounts and returns so that I can refine our pricing and discount policies.

- **US-S3-CFO-3**  
  As a CFO, I want visibility into payment delays (DSO) by customer so that I can adjust credit limits and terms.

- **US-S3-CEO-1**  
  As a CEO, I want a single dashboard combining revenue, on-time delivery, and margin by product line so that I can track overall performance at a glance.

- **US-S3-COO-1**  
  As a COO, I want scenario views that show the impact of a 10% demand jump or a 5-day supplier delay so that I can plan capacity and buffer stock.

- **US-S3-COO-2**  
  As a COO, I want to compare plant performance on throughput, on-time delivery, and scrap so that I can prioritize improvement projects.
